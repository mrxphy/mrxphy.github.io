Mind Over Matter

Welcome to our site, we are Mind Over Matter. We are on a mission to make the world a happier place. Many people feel trapped and believe that there is no one there for them, but we want to show that this is not the case. We have more information here.

Why it matters

One in three adults in the USA struggle with depression. The depression rates among adults in the United States jumped from 8.5% to 32.8% in the last 3 years. These stats aren't something we can just ignore. A lot more people than we think suffer with depression and it is becoming a huge problem in a lot of countries, which is why we want to work together to help make the world a happier place.

How we fundraise

LAs a mental health charity called Mind Over Matter, we are always looking for ways to raise money for mental health research and support. One way we do this is by organizing fundraisers in the community. These fundraisers can take many forms, such as bake sales, car washes, and sponsored events like runs or walks. We work closely with local businesses and organizations to secure sponsorships and donations, and we also rely on the support of volunteers to help plan and execute these events. By organizing fundraisers in the community, we are able to raise much-needed funds to support our mission of improving mental health awareness and access to care. We are grateful to all of the members of the community who support our efforts and help us to make a difference in the lives of those who are struggling with mental health issues.
