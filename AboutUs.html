About Us

We are a not for profit charitable organisation based in Dublin, Ireland

We raise awareness and lobby for the mental health of young people in Ireland. We also provide help to those struggling with their mental health.


Our Mission Statement


Our mission as a mental health charity is to raise awareness and provide support for those struggling with mental health issues. We believe that mental health is just as important as physical health and that everyone deserves access to quality care and treatment. Through our fundraisers and community events, we aim to break the stigma surrounding mental health and provide resources and support to those in need. We work closely with local organizations and mental health professionals to ensure that our efforts are making a positive impact in the lives of those we serve. We are dedicated to promoting mental wellness and providing a supportive community for those who need it most. We believe that by working together, we can make a real difference in the fight against mental illness and improve the lives of countless individuals.
